## Reliable Power Synchronization
Synchronization Panels are essential for ensuring a continuous and stable power supply by synchronizing multiple power sources, such as generators and utility mains. ESL’s Synchronization Panels are designed to ensure continuous power flow by synchronizing multiple power sources, such as generators or utility mains. Built to handle up to 32 power sources, these panels provide automatic load balancing and protection against voltage irregularities. Whether it’s for data centers, hospitals, or manufacturing plants, ESL’s Synchronization Panels deliver reliable power, reducing the risk of outages and costly downtime.
**Features:**
  * **Multi-Source Synchronization:** Syncs up to 32 power sources for continuous supply.
  * **Protection Against Voltage Fluctuations:** Guards against overvoltage, undervoltage, and phase loss.
  * **Automatic Load Sharing:** Distributes loads evenly to optimize power usage and reduce costs.
  * **Easy Configuration:** Quick setup and integration with existing systems.


**Applications:** Ideal for data centers, health care facilities, manufacturing plants, and other critical infrastructure.
**Quality Assurance:** Built to IEC 61439 standards and ISO 9001 certified, ensuring that your power systems operate with maximum reliability.
**Technical Data:**
  * Forms of Segregation: Available in Forms 1, 2, 3, and 4
  * Enclosure Type: Mild steel, stainless steel, polycarbonate
  * IP Rating: IP44 to IP66
  * Mounting Mode: Wall mount & floor standing
