## Versatile Power Distribution
Power Distribution Panels are the central hub of any electrical system, managing the flow of electricity to various circuits. ESL’s panels are highly customizable, with options for cascaded circuit protection, remote monitoring, and scalable configurations. Built to handle up to 6300A, these panels are engineered for reliability, safety, and efficiency.
**Features:**
  * **Cascaded Circuit Protection:** Multi-layered safety for electrical systems. With overcurrent, undervoltage and overvoltage protection
  * **Remote Monitoring:** Real-time status updates and energy usage tracking.
  * **Customizable Configurations:** Tailored to meet specific operational needs.
  * **Scalability:** Easily integrates additional circuits or features as needed.
  * Varied enclosure options available, with respect to size, ingress protection (IP), most appropriate materials for protection against harsh environmental factors such as humidity, corrosion, highly flammable environments etc.
  * Our Distribution boards can be designed with any form of separation required (1,2,3,4)
  * Designed with special components such as phase monitoring, over-voltage monitoring relays, smart meters, humidity and flood sensors.


**Applications:** Suited for commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and residential complexes requiring robust and reliable power distribution.
**Quality Assurance:** Built according to IEC 61439 standards and ISO 9001 certified for maximum reliability.
