# COVID-19 Policy
The Automation Ghana Group (TAGG) remains committed to the safety and wellbeing of its workers, customers and visitors. This commitment has been demonstrated through our high standards of environmental protection; the sharing of best practices; and provision a safe and healthy work environment. With the recent threat of the novel corona virus (COVID-19) and its potential ramifications, there is no higher priority TAGG than the safety of our customers, our partners, our employees, and their families.
To this effect, the management of TAGG from the inception of the outbreak outlined measures and implemented amendments to procedures in alignment with the threat assessment protocols of the World Health Organization (WHO) and regional government agencies in a bid to help curb the spread of the virus whiles ensuring the safety and wellbeing of employees and other stakeholders. These have been outlined in the TAGG COVID-19 management plan.
The management of TAGG, going forward, commits to stand by all procedures and measures outlined and implemented and will continue to provide all needed resources to see to its successful implementation and sustenance. We also commit to the continual monitoring of outputs and review at regular intervals to ensure that policies are delivering on intended outputs.
Finally, legislatives and measures will continually be monitored for changes and our policies and measures reviewed to reflect them accordingly.
