## Power Factor Correction
Power Factor Correction (PFC) Panels are designed to optimize the power factor of your electrical systems, reducing energy consumption and lowering operational costs. ESL’s Power Factor Correction (PFC) Panels help you do just that by improving the power factor of your electrical system. By minimizing reactive power, these panels reduce demand charges and energy waste, directly lowering your electricity costs. Moreover, they protect your equipment from damage caused by poor power quality, extending its operational lifespan. Designed for industrial plants, commercial facilities, and other high-energy environments, ESL’s PFC Panels ensure your systems run at maximum efficiency.
**Features:**
  * **Automatic & Manual Operation:** Flexible power factor management options.
  * **High Current Handling Capability:** Suitable for large-scale industrial applications.
  * **Reactive Power Reduction:** Minimizes energy wastage and demand charges.
  * **Improved Equipment Longevity:** Reduces strain on electrical systems, enhancing durability.


**Applications:** Perfect for manufacturing plants, commercial buildings, and facilities with high inductive loads.
**Quality Assurance:** Built according to IEC 61439 standards and ISO 9001 certified, ensuring effective and reliable power factor correction.
**Technical Data:**
  * Forms of Segregation: Available in Forms 1, 2, 3, and 4
  * Enclosure Type: Mild steel, stainless steel, polycarbonate
  * IP Rating: IP44 to IP66
  * Mounting Mode: Wall mount & floor standing
